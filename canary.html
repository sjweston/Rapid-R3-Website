Canary in the coal mine

Representativeness disclaimer

These analyses are based on responses collected from 1042 caregivers between the dates of June 01, 2020 and June 04, 2020. These caregivers represent a range of voices: 8.64% are Black/African American, 21.31% are LatinX, and 26.07% live at or below 1.5 times the federal poverty line. Proportions/percentages are calculated based on the item-level response rates, not out of the total sample size. The data for these analyses are not weighted.
