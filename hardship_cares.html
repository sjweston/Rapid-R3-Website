Material hardship post CARES

Representativeness disclaimer

These analyses are based on responses collected from 7660 caregivers between the dates of April 06, 2020 and August 27, 2020. These caregivers represent a range of voices: 10.35% are Black/African American, 20.17% are LatinX, and 20.81% live at or below 1.5 times the federal poverty line. Proportions/percentages are calculated based on the item-level response rates, not out of the total sample size. The data for these analyses are not weighted.
