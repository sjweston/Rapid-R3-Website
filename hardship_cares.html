Material hardship post CARES

Representativeness disclaimer

These analyses are based on responses collected from 7312 caregivers between the dates of April 06, 2020 and October 08, 2020. These caregivers represent a range of voices: 9.38% are Black/African American, 19.08% are LatinX, and 29.13% live at or below 1.5 times the federal poverty line. Proportions/percentages are calculated based on the item-level response rates, not out of the total sample size. The data for these analyses are not weighted.
