Discrimination and concerns for children

Representativeness disclaimer

These analyses are based on responses collected from 1909 caregivers between the dates of August 25, 2020 and September 24, 2020. These caregivers represent a range of voices: 9.06% are Black/African American, 15.56% are LatinX, (0.52% report being both Black/African American and LatinX,) and 24.93% live at or below 1.5 times the federal poverty line. Proportions/percentages are calculated based on the item-level response rates, not out of the total sample size. The data for these analyses are not weighted.
