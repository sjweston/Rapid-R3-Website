Representativeness disclaimer

These analyses are based on responses collected from 1549 caregivers between the dates of August 25, 2020 and September 10, 2020. These caregivers represent a range of voices: 9.94% are Black/African American, 15.17% are LatinX, and 23.76% live at or below 1.5 times the federal poverty line. Proportions/percentages are calculated based on the item-level response rates, not out of the total sample size. The data for these analyses are not weighted.
